    Download and upload

    Downloading and exporting uploading block definitions from the forms designer

    Building block defintions can be imported and exported from the forms designer should you wish to share a building block between different instances of forms4health.

    Download

    To download a building block definition first find the building block on the building blocks page, then click the download icon.

    Upload

    To upload simply click the upload button on the building block page and navigate to your downloaded building block on your machine. Once complete you will see the new building block in the list and an “Upload success” message will appear.
